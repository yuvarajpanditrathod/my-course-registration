About KLE Technological University

KLE Technological University, Hubballi (formerly known as B.V.Bhoomaraddi College of Engineering & Technology) is a premier institution established in 1947. The university is recognized for excellence in technical education and research.

Our BE Computer Science program is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge and practical skills in cutting-edge technologies, preparing students for successful careers in the IT industry.
